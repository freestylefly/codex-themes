# Layout Catalog

Use these seven layouts exactly. Reference images are in `assets/layout-references/`.

## dream-banner

A wide hero banner across the top of the Codex home screen. The main content sits below the banner. Safe area for text is the left two-thirds of the banner; keep the subject on the right or softly blurred.

## split-studio

Hero fills roughly the left half, content sits on the right. Good for asymmetric images with strong vertical subject on the left.

## full-canvas

Hero covers the entire background behind a translucent content layer. Use atmospheric, low-detail imagery; increase `scrim` so text remains readable.

## terminal-grid

A dense, grid-aware layout. Prefer high contrast, low radius, mono font, minimal decoration. Hero is still present but smaller and more structured.

## paper-board

A light, tactile layout with low glass and subtle note-like cards. Warm neutrals work well; keep decoration moderate and glass off.

## minimal-focus

Maximum whitespace, small centered hero, large typography. Use calm, low-saturation images; keep effects minimal.

## retro-messenger

A dense late-2000s desktop messenger layout with a two-row blue title/toolbar,
sharp one-pixel borders, a compact right information rail, and a squared
composer. Use a light cool palette, `none` or `sm` radius, compact density,
system fonts, no glass, minimal shadow, and a transparent mascot-style hero.
